# Privacy Policy — YouTube Screenshot Extension **Effective Date:** January 8, 2026 **Version:** 1.0 YouTube Screenshot is a Chrome extension that allows users to capture screenshots from YouTube videos with one click. Your privacy is very important to us. ## 1. Data Collection This extension does **NOT** collect, store, sell, or share any personal or sensitive user data. - No personal information is collected - No browsing history is tracked - No keystrokes or interactions are logged - Screenshots are not uploaded to any server ## 2. Permissions Usage The extension uses the following Chrome permission: - **storage** — Used only to save user preferences such as toggle states. No personal or identifiable data is stored. ## 3. Screenshot Handling Screenshots are generated locally within your browser and saved directly to your device or clipboard. No screenshot content is transmitted externally. ## 4. Third-Party Services This extension does not use third-party APIs, analytics tools, ads, or tracking technologies. ## 5. Children’s Privacy This extension does not target children and does not knowingly collect data from users of any age. ## 6. Policy Updates This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated version and effective date. ## 7.
