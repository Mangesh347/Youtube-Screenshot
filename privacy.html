Privacy Policy

YouTube Screenshot Extension

Last updated: 2026

YouTube Screenshot is a Chrome extension that allows users to capture screenshots from YouTube videos and control related UI features. Your privacy is very important to us.

1. Data Collection

This extension does NOT collect, store, sell, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

  • No personal information is collected
  • No browsing history is tracked
  • No keystrokes are logged
  • No screenshots are uploaded

2. Permissions Usage

The extension uses Chrome permissions only to provide core functionality.

  • storage – Used to save user preferences such as toggle states and screenshot format.

3. Screenshot Handling

Screenshots are generated locally inside your browser and are saved directly to your device or clipboard. No data is transmitted externally.

4. Third-Party Services

This extension does not use third-party APIs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or tracking technologies.

5. Children’s Privacy

Screenshot YouTube does not target children and does not knowingly collect data from users of any age.

6. Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store listing.
